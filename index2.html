Our Services

asset management and early prevention

Specialists in the drafting and execution of wills, trusts and other estate planning instruments, all prepared according to the specific needs of each family. We create advance directives to determine the custody of minors in the event of the parents' incapacity, protecting the assets and particular interests for each case.

Representation in succession matters

We offer specialized advice on international inheritances, providing effective and strategic solutions to protect your rights and interests, building bridges between countries, ensuring reliable professional management of each legal situation.

Advance planning

Preparation and study of powers of representation valid even in the event of death, living will and advance directives to guarantee that your will is respected in different nations.

Visa processing in the USA

Together with specialized legal firms, we advise and process investment, talent, professional and medical visas globally.

Transnational Legal Services in Commercial Contracting

We advise and draft commercial contracts anticipating litigation in compliance with the regulations of Argentina and the United States. Together with American colleagues we ensure a legal vision, comprehensive and adapted to both legal systems.

Interdisciplinary Management in Migration and Family Support

We provide complete support in the migration process for our clients and their families with personalized attention. We have an interdisciplinary team that ensures comprehensive support.

About Us

At Anticipate Legal LLC, we offer high-quality, personalized legal services, backed by more than 27 years of experience. Founded by Cecilia Christello and Mónica Baldi, our firm is distinguished by its deep knowledge and training in law, guaranteeing direct, personalized and confidential attention without intermediaries. We partner with law firms in the USA, specialized in immigration, contracts and successions, which allows us to offer comprehensive and precise advice. As mothers and professionals, we bring empathy and dedication, especially in family law and prevention when entering into contracts.

Mónica Baldi

Graduated as a lawyer with honors in 1996 from the University of Buenos Aires. She has a postgraduate degree in Family Systemic Psychology, completed a Specialization in Family, Childhood and Adolescence Law and was certified as a mediator at the same University. She is certified as a paralegal in all areas of Law at the University of Miami. Active member of the Center for Research, Prevention and Assistance for Minors at Risk (CIPAMER).

Cecilia Christello

Graduated as a lawyer from the University of Buenos Aires in 1993 with a focus on private law. He completed a postgraduate degree in Health Law and Specialization in Family, Childhood and Adolescence Law, both at Buenos Aires University.
